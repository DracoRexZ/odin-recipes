Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken Alfredo is a popular pasta dish consisting of
a cheesy alfredo sauce, usually accompanied by chicken.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Fettuccine or linguini pasta
  • Butter
  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Garlic

Directions

  1. Boil the noodles al dente, or as described in the packaging

  2. Cook the chicken. Season then pan-fry in oil and butter for about
    6 minutes on each side, or until the internal temperature is
    165 degrees fahrenheit. Transfer to a cutting board, and cut down
    to about 1/2 inch slices.

  3. Make the sauce. Put the cream, butter, and seasonings into the same
    pan used for the chicken. Stir until thickened, then add Parmesan.

  4. Assemble and serve! Your pasta is ready for assemblage! Toss the
    pasta in the Alfredo sauce, then add chicken. Garnish is optional.
