Kraft Mac and Cheese

Description

Kraft Mac and Cheese is a simple, quick dish that doesn't require
any external ingredients. Perfect for a quick meal!

Ingredients

  • Box of Kraft Mac and Cheese
  • 1/4 stick of butter
  • 1/4 cup of milk

Directions

  1. Remove the cheese packet and boil noodles. Use about
    6 cups of water.

  2. Add milk, butter, and cheese mix. Strain the noodles,
    then place them in the pot again. Add the cheese packet,
    butter, and milk.

  3. Stir well. Stir until the butter is completely melted,
    and the cheese is completely mixed.

  4. Enjoy!
