Gingerbread Cookies

Description

Gingerbread cookies are a popular snack during the holidays;
so much so, they've become a staple of December.

Ingredients

  • Ground ginger
  • Cinnamon
  • Cloves
  • Molasses
  • Sugar cookie icing

Directions

  1. Using a hand mixer in a large bowl, beat butter, brown sugar,
    and molasses until it's fluffy. Then, add egg and vanilla and beat
    until combined.

  2. Whisk flour, spices, baking soda, and salt until combined in a
    medium bowl. Gradually add dry ingredients to wet ingredients. This
    includes fresh ginger, orange zest, and black pepper. Don't overmix.

  3. Divide dough in half, then create two discs. Wrap each in plastic
    and chill for about 3 hours.

  4. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Line two large baking sheets. Place
    a disc on a flat surface and roll until 1/4 inch. Cut out gingerbread
    designs and place on baking sheets.

  5. Bake until slightly puffed. Let cool on baking sheets for 5 minutes,
    then transfer to cooling rack.

  6. Repeat with the other disc. Once finished, decorate cookies with icing.
